Belladoota kan ilaali Look at the animals Regardez les animaux

Jenny Katz

Sandy Campbell

Demoze Degefa

Oromo

Level 1

Belladoota kan ilaali.

Look at the animals.

Regardez les animaux.

Saani tuni 'moo' jetti.

The cow says, "Moo."

La vache dit, « Meuh. »

Ra'een kuni 'me-ee' jedha.

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

La chèvre dit, « Bêê, bêê. »

Fardi kuni 'himmi' jedha.

The horse says, "Neigh."

Le cheval dit, « Hiiii. »

Boyyeen kuni 'gurruu' jedha.

The pig says, "Grunt."

Le cochon dit, « Groin, groin. »

Lukkuun tuni 'kuku-kuu' jetti.

The chicken says, "Cluck."

La poule dit, « Cot-cot. »

Sareen kuni 'wuuuwuu' jedha.

The dog says, "Woof."

Le chien dit, « Wouf. »

Qotee bulaan kuni 'ohuu' jedha.

The farmer says, "Shhh!"

Le fermier dit, « Chut ! »

Written by: Jenny Katz
Illustrated by: Sandy Campbell
Translated by: Demoze Degefa
Language: Oromo
Level 1
