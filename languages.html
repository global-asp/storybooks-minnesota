Languages

The following languages are currently included in the scope of the Storybooks Minnesota project:

  • English
  • Amharic
  • Arabic
  • Cambodian
  • French
  • German
  • Hmong
  • Karen
  • Lao
  • Mandarin
  • Norwegian
  • Oromo
  • Russian
  • Somali
  • Spanish
  • Swedish
  • Vietnamese
We are always looking for translators! If you speak one of the above languages and would like to help us translate stories, please contact us at globalafricanstorybook [at] gmail [dot] com
